Pricing Formula Legend & Notation

Variables

Proc = Procurement Qty (Required Qty from BOM explosion)
Avail = Available Stock (closing stock from SAP warehouses)
LPP  = Last Purchase Price (SAP OITM.LastPurPrc)
NPP  = New Purchase Price (from admin monthly price upload)
Net  = Net Required = MAX(0, Proc − Avail)

Column Formulas

Old Stock Price Total   = MIN(Avail, Proc) × LPP
New Purchase Price Total = Net × NPP
Weighted Avg Price     = (Old Stock Price Total + New Purchase Price Total) ÷ Proc

Case Rules for Weighted Avg Price

CASE 1 Avail ≥ Proc → Weighted Avg = LPP (stock covers full requirement; no new purchase needed)
CASE 2 Avail = 0 and NPP > 0 → Weighted Avg = NPP (zero stock, must buy everything at new price)
CASE 3 Avail = 0 and NPP = 0/missing → Weighted Avg = LPP (zero stock, no new price available; fallback to last known price)
CASE 4 0 < Avail < Proc and NPP > 0 → Weighted Avg = (Avail×LPP + Net×NPP) ÷ Proc (blended: partial stock at old price, remainder at new price)
CASE 5 0 < Avail < Proc and NPP = 0/missing → Weighted Avg = LPP (partial stock, no new price; fallback to LPP)

Notes

• LPP comes from SAP's OITM.LastPurPrc field (last recorded purchase price).
• NPP is loaded from the admin monthly price Excel (ItemCode → Price sheet).
• Total Price column = Old Stock Price Total + New Purchase Price Total.
• Weighted Avg is used downstream in the Per-FG Cost tab for non-PX items.

Per-FG Cost Methodology & Formulas

Inputs

WAP(code) = Weighted Avg Price per unit of any non-PX item (5-case formula from closing stock + new prices)
PXPrice(px) = Provided via PX Price Excel upload (ItemCode → Price)
BOM(fg) = All ingredient rows for that FG from the BOM explosion (run via /get_bom_only)
FG_Qty = Projected production quantity for the FG (from Step 1 Excel)

WAP — 5 Case Rules (non-PX items)

C1Avail ≥ Proc → WAP = Last Purchase Price
C2Avail = 0, New Price exists → WAP = New Purchase Price
C3Avail = 0, no New Price → WAP = Last Purchase Price (fallback)
C4Partial stock, New Price exists → WAP = (Avail×LPP + Net×NPP) ÷ Proc
C5Partial stock, no New Price → WAP = Last Purchase Price (fallback)

PX Items

PXCost(px, fg) = PXPrice(px) × Item_Projection(px, fg)
• If a PX code has no price in the uploaded Excel it is included with ₹0 value.
• The MD formula breakdown (from /px_formula_costs) is fetched for reference only.

Per-FG Aggregation

TotalFGValue(fg) = Σ [ WAP(item) × Item_Projection(item) ] for all items in BOM
PerUnitCost(fg) = TotalFGValue(fg) ÷ FG_Qty
• Item_Projection already incorporates FG_Qty (it is scaled at BOM explosion time).
• So the division by FG_Qty normalises to a true per-unit figure.
